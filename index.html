I find beauty in the most basic elements of life and have a tenacious appetite for transforming simple objects into emotional concepts involving familiar relationships that transcend cultural and geographical boundaries. I believe that human emotion is the most important language we all share, galvanizing us with a complex range of feelings that form the building blocks of every relationship. My goal in art is to interpret the human experience by capturing moments of raw emotion and representing them visually in the most basic form to express a unique point of view and establish a connection with the viewer. The creative process continually evolves after I have a basis for an idea, but the most important step remains the use of sketches that transpose mental notes gathered throughout the days into thoughtful concepts without limitations. In my sketchbook, I never have a bad idea that isn’t erasable.
I challenge myself artistically to be as creative as possible when interpreting a realistic concept and choose simple lines and form to create a harmonious balance between geometric and organic shapes. By exploring new forms of expression and listening to the needs and desires of the art pieces, I am constantly evolving and maturing as an artist. Hopefully, my works are thought provoking and beautiful with an excursion into the unknown, inspiring conversations and sparking emotions in all. My art will continue to dominate my path in life with my best work yet to come.
I appreciate your time and any comments or questions are welcome.
